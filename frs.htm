Dr Colin Windsor FRS: Royal Society Citation March 1995

Dr Windsor is distinguished for his outstanding experimental and modeling skills, used to especial effect in the industrial application of beams of neutrons. He demonstrated the use of pulsed beams of neutrons to study solid matter, and pioneered the application of the penetrating abilities of neutrons to study critical defects and internal stress in engineering products. More recently he has applied pattern recognition techniques and neural nets to a range of areas, including weld defect classification, the analysis of the financial index, and signature recognition.
