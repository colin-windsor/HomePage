19. Still, there is sadness

One cannot escape pain

There are always reasons for it

And it will come again

Every time that you are hit.

~

Don’t be fooled by me

My pain is disguised by this veil

Although I seem happy

You don’t hear my inside wail.

~

For love makes fools of us

Pain’s there where it was not before

We’re trapped into lust

If just leads to wanting more.

~

Your life changes so much

With sad songs that each night you hear

You try to hide love’s touch

But you can’t disguise a fear.

~

All you do I think, dream

Hoping that your ell not get hurt.

At night you kick and scream

You mind makes your heart alert.

I’d do so much for your

I know now what for you I feel

For myself, I am true

Yet I wonder if it’s real
