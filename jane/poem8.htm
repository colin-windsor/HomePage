8. I'll Never say Goodbye

I'll never say goodbye!

A doorway opens in my life

He stands there, and I want him.

Don't go! I think, I need you so!

Like God, I want to worship you

And every day, like fools we go

About our lives, and love, and hope

And wanting you to know.

~

The sinking of my heart in vain

I wish my face weren't shown

For tears of love, and heart, and pain

Seem from my heart to moan.

I look up and all that face

No! I think, I won't say it!

I'll never say goodbye to you.

~

2/4/90
