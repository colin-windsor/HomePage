16. Remember me

Go about your life

With a smile to end your strife

Free me in your mind

After times when you were kind.

Remember me, remember me

Don’t you ever set me free

Those times when you’re blue

When you know not what to do

Get me in your head

And put on a smile instead.

Remember me, remember me

I only belong to thee

I am yours eternally

If you’ll just remember me.

~

19/6/90
