17. To you, my dearest

Oh my love, how you shine, how you shine!

You to me are so divine

I hope that you will be mine

For you know not what you mean to me

~

Oh my love, happiness, happiness

O so much you would not guess

When you give me nothing less

Than undying love that’s ever there.

~

Oh my love, I love you, I love you

An I know you want me too

It is so, what can I do?

But just keep loving for ever.

~

20/6/90
