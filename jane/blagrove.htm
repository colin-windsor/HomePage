From the OXFORD MAIL 30th December 1992

Naturists are about to bare all at a secluded beauty spot. The 12-acre woodland site at Boars Hill has just been bought by the Oxford Naturists' Club for £20,000. Its 220 members have spent years scouring the county for a permanent site. Club member Mr Leonard Harris said they have low-key plans for Blagrove Copse. "The club does not expect more than 30 people at a time to use the woods. We want to eventually put a small clubhouse there and a car park for about a dozen cars. But we are taking things very gently and talking to local people. This site is exactly what we wanted though."
