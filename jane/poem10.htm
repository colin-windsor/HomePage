10. The light still shines on you.

I wake up, and think

Another day of pain

Of knowing you’re not there.

But I’ll never forget you

Whatever I have to do

To show how much I care

You don’t know how much

You mean to me. I’m there.

You made me so happy

It put me to shame

And even memories fade

They’ll never come again.

Now all my days are rainy

Love pouring from my eyes

And heart that’s lost- guide me by

The light that shines on you.

~

2/4/90
