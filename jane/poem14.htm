14. Come play with me

Come, play with me in sand

Let us go hand in hand

So we can skip and sing

And see happiness ring.

~

Why should I be so sad

When with you I am glad,

And laughs and smiles are here

For I know you are near.

~

Let us not waste today

When there are games to play

So I can love you more

If I loved you before.

~

18/6/90
