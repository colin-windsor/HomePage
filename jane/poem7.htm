7. Painful Moments

In God's eyes - he is perfect

He has it all - It's him

His eyes are there to melt me

His hands so soft and gentle.

~

And he is always there for me

Through painful moments alone

His warmth spreads all around

Like butter, calming, soothing.

~

And if tears spring to my yes

They're gone, with one brush of his hand

For pain and tears should not be there

For where he, is joy.

~

2/4/90
