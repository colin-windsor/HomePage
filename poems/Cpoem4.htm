Dark minutes

Two lives

Two lives, two minds, onelove, one year
Reaching out together to fine new joys
Our little morris and our tent release our ties
Ucaster, Studland, CLivedon, Crete are ours

And from our open bodies, new lives open out
Our friends from OXNAT encluse us with their hearts
Yoga, swimming, life-clas, all refresh our days
And lovely Blagrove, round your figure, fills my gaze

by Colin Windsor: May 1995?
