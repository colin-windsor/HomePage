Dark minutes

Morning come, my love is there
Gentle touch to bodies bare
Breakfast healthy, mousli, egg
Better start no man could beg

Down the ping pong, up the net
Crash the balls: you win I bet!
On the fire: off the clothes
Exercises warm our toes

Happy singing later on
Friendship true music has won
Bed-y-byes, no pollow fights
Perfect days for perfect nights
