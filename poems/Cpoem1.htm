Dark minutes

Welcome

Oh tiny babe, conceived in joy
So deep inside your human home
We welcome you

Long Acre's plot
Its warmth, its grass, its flowers
Is your sweet world

Your family will gather round
And sing and laugh
And play with you

So precious miracle
Of ever-multiplying cells
We wait for you

by Colin Windsor: January 1992
