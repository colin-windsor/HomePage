Happy Christmas 2025

It was raining for our Christmas carols zt Hagbourne Church last year. We all moved inside.

On 30/12/24 the Windsors decided to keep our La Bruyere house in the family.

In March, Mo visited Justin and Sigrid at their house by the beach near Edinburgh at Mussleborough.

In May Mo gave an organ recital at Hagbourne Church as part of a series to show off our gorgeous organ. Colin gave one in July, with the family singing his old compositions.

In July we had some very sociable times with our good friends at La Bruyere. We shall miss them.

Our CCC choir went to Bristol in August. We went by train. The programme included lovely music old and new. This is our conductor Alex at the organ.

In September we took the train to Plymouth and stayed is a guest house near the Hoe.
