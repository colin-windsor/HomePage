Happy Christmas 2023

Snow around our Hagbourne church

In February grand-daughter Alice and Ed were married in a superb service with amazing music at Lichfield Cathedral. There followed lots of dancing!

May morning was celebrated with song and Bucks Fizz atop Dorchester Abbey

In June, Mo’s 80th birthday was celebrated at home with lots of family and friends ending with a big curry!

In July we took a French holiday at a splendid B&B in the nearest town we had never visited: Sille le Guillaume.

In August our CCC choir sang in Chester Cathedral. Mo visited her old haunts where she used to live.

In October the Windsor Warblers again contributed to Mo’s Autumn Concert in our Church.

We continued to green our house with 7 watt LED lamps powered by little 12V batteries from our neighbour. The base was from Belleme forest floor.

Wishing you all the best for a

Happy Christmas
