"Fifteenth 2' Great"

Not much used on its own, this bright sound adds to the 8' Open Diaphason and 4' Principal to give the main great sound played second.

The second sounds are with the 4' Principal along with the 8' Open Diaphason .

The third sounds are the 2' Fifteenth along with the soft 8' Dulciana. It gives a quiet bright sound suitable for faster pieces.
