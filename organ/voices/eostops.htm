The stops on our home digital Organ

The upper picture shows the electronic organ console showing the Great draw stops and the electic push-button stops under the Great keyboard,

The present organ includes the stop shown in the table. Click on each stop to hear its sound. When the new page appears, click on the player "Start" arrow. For the Great and Swell the first four stops in each list are the mechanical drawstops. The others, including the all the Pedal stops are operated by the push buttons. The 16' Double Trumpet and the Pedal Schalmei 4' are not illustrated.
