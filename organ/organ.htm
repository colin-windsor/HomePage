Organ Playing

I have been deputising on the church organ ever since the 1960s at Steventon Church. I was choirmaster at Goring Church for several years in the 1980s.

In May 2007 I begain playing on the second sunday of the month at St John's Whitchurch Hill. In October 2012 I became official organist, playing on the first and third Sundays at the lovely St Mary's Church by the river at Whitchurch. I retired after my 80th birthday with a splendid service and party afterwards. Now I enjoy playing occasionally at Upton village church. I might be available to play for occasional Sunday services, or other occasions.

Mo and I play a digital electronic organ at home using some of the old Binns organ from Streatley Church.
