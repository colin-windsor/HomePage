"The Lord is Gracious": An introductory sentence for solo and choir

This song was written on 27/1/89 for Goring Church choir. The words are Psalm 145 verses 8 and 9 form the Introductory Sentence for the Seventh Sunday before Easter in the 1980 Alternative Service Book:

"The Lord is gracious and merciful:
And his compassion is over all that he has made."
