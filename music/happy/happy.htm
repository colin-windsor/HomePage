"Happy is the man": A short antham for five solos and choir

This song was written in January 1988 for Goring Church choir. The words are from Proverbs 3 13-17 as in the 1980 Alternative Service Book:

"Happy is the man who finds wisdom,
And the man who gets understanding,
For the gain from it is better that gain from silver
And its profit better than gold.
She is more precious than jewels,
And nothing you can desire can be compared with her.
Long life is in her right hand;
In her left hand are riches and honour.
Her ways are ways of pleasantness,
And all her paths are peace."

The original soloists were, in order:
Elizabeth Windsor (treble)
Colin Windsor (Tenor)
Diane Smith (Alto)
Colonel Merry (Bass)
Mary Seymour (Soprano)
