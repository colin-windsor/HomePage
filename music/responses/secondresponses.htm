The second set of responses set to the Prayer Book words by Colin Windsor

O Lord show thy mercy upon us: And grant us thy salvation.

O Lord save the Queen: And mercifully hear us when we call upon Thee.

Endue thy ministers with righteousness: And make Thy chosen people joyful.

O Lord save they people: And bless thine inheriance.

Give peace in our time, O Lord: Because there is none other thay fighteth for us, but only Thou O God.

O God make clean our hearts within us: And take not Thy Holy Spirit from us: Amen.
