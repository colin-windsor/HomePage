"Clear and Cool": A song for a theatrical production

This song was written in October 1991 for a panto production by Goring Players produced by Mary xx of the Water Babies by Charles Kingsley.

"Clear and cool, clear and cool,
By laughing shallow and dreamy pool,
Cool and clear, cool and clear,
By sining shingle, and foaming weir.

Under the crag where the ousel sings,
And the ivied wall where the church bells ring
Undefiled for the undefiled
Play by me, bathe in me, mother and child"
