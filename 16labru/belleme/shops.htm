Belleme: The Main shops

The main supermarket is in St Martin 1km from the house, on the junction between the by-pass and the road to Mammers. It is a Carrefour store and opens at 8.00am

The biggest supermarket in Belleme town is the Marche Plus, on Rue Boucicant, which opens at 7.00am

Belleme is generally vey well served by smaller shops
