16, La Bruyere, St Martin du Vieux Belleme, 61130, Normandy, France

"Pavillon de St Louis" in Belleme forest

It is a short cycle ride (~7km) to visit this 19C hunting lodge. Cycle up through La Bruyere, then turn left at the top towards La Perriere. Then at the bottom of a dip where you meet a metalled road, cycle (walk!) up the hill until you meet a crossing track where you turn right. In a km you meet the "Carrefour du Chene Saint Louis". The pavillon is on your right.
