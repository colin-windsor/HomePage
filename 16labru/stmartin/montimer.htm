The chateau de Montimer at La Perriere

It is a lovely (7km each way) cycle ride to visit this our local "Chateau". It is a private house and you cannot go in. Cycle up into the forest and turn left for La Perriere. As you descend steeply into the town, turn right at the T junction and it is about 100m further on on your left. You can make a circular trip by returning via Bellevilliers.
