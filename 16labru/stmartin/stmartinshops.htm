The shops at St Martin

The big Champions supermarket is in St Martin 1km from the house. It is about 1km and easy to cycle or walk. Go down the road, turn right to go through the village. It is on the junction between the by-pass and the road to Mammers. It opens at 8.00am.

There is also a big "MAG"s Hardware store on the same site.
