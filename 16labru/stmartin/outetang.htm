16, La Bruyere, St Martin du Vieux Belleme, 61130, Normandy, France

The "Etang de la Herse" in Belleme forest.

An easy cycle ride up into the forest. Turn right at the top of the hill, and go East until you reach the main road to Montagne. Turn left here and go down to the bottom of the hill. Next to the Herse Roman spring is a fine lake "L'Etang de la Herse".

It is surrounded by hilly woods with many paths. It is good for fishing and for picnics.
