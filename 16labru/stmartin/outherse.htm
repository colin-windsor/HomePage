16, La Bruyere, St Martin du Vieux Belleme, 61130, Normandy, France

The ancient Roman spring at la Herse" in Belleme forest.

An easy cycle ride up into the forest and across to the Montagne road soon brings you to the old Roman spring called "La Herse".

There is a noticeboard with all the details in French. The spring itself does not look too clear, but it is reputed to have magical medicinal even aphrodisiac powers.

There is a lovely octagonal wooden house near the spring.
