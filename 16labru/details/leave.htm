When you leave

1. Shut all the shutters: bedrooms, lounge and back door

2. Make sure that all the bedroom and loft doors are shut

3. Lock the back door

4. Stop the boiler by pressing the red knob

5. Close the gas to boiler with 1/2 turn on knob nearby

6. Close the gas stove with 1/2 turn on knob behind stove

7. Clean fridge and switch off leaving the fridge door open

8. Shut off water in lounge near the TV

9. Shut off electricity in lounge near front door

10. In cold weather, draw all the curtains

11. Lock the front door just the key (leave the upper bolt open)

We hope you had a good stay!
