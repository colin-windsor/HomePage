When you leave

1. Shut all the shutters: bedrooms, loft room, lounge and back door

2. Make sure that all the bedroom and loft doors are shut for fire reasons

3. Make sure the back door secondary glass in in place. Lock the back door and put the key on the hook just to the right of the door. (The spare key will not work if the key is in place)

4. Stop the boiler by pressing mains button

5. Close the gas to boiler with 1/2 turn on yellow knob under the boiler

6. Close the gas stove with 1/2 turn on knob behind stove

7. Clean fridge and switch off leaving the fridge door open

8. Shut off water in lounge near the TV

9. Shut off electricity in lounge near front door

10. In cold weather, draw all the curtains

11. Lock the front door just the key (leave the upper bolt open)

We hope you had a good stay!
