James's Microwave

This has many knobs that you may not need.

To set the time: Press "Clock"

Then enter the hours and minutes according to the table. For:
10 hours1 hour
10 m1 m
Press:
10 min1 min
10 sec1 sec

Then press "Clock" again.

For ordinary heating: Set time on the buttons: Press "Start"

It pings when it finishes or press cancel and open the door when ready.

If things do not seem to work properly: turn off at the mains.
