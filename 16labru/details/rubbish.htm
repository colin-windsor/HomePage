Rubbish collection

There is no household rubbish collection.

Rubbish should be taken to the local dechet which is only a few hundred metres away. Go down the road to the cross roads and turn north. In 50m on your right is the local dechet. There are bins for: a) All glass things b) Cardboard, plastic bottles and tins for recycling c) Bagged household waste. This must be <40 litres and in a black plastic bag. They are placed in the round containers. To open these you need the special electronic card placed in the kitchen draw to the left of the steps on the North side. Larger things should be taken to the "Dechetterie" or tip collection point marked on the Belleme map. It is manned during the day

If you are driving home via Bellavillier there are convenient collection points just before you approach the juction before the town.
