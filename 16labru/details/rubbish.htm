Rubbish

There is no rubbish collection day any more. Any glass, metal, paper, cardboard and plastic can be taken down to the local dechett. Go down the road to town and turn left. On you right in 50m is the dechett area. General kitchen waste in a black bag can be loaded into the separate cylindrical containers, but these require an ID card left in the kitchen to the right hand side of the steps.

Larger things should be taken to the "Dechetterie" or tip collection point marked on the Belleme map. It is manned during the day

If you are driving home via Bellavillier there are convenient collection points just before you approach the juction before the town.
