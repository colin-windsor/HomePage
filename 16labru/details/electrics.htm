Problems with the electrics

Plugs coming loose: They are not screwed in to the wall but clamped against the socket and are prone to come loose.

When you pull on the plug hold the socket in as you pull

It is fairly easy to correct if they are badly loose

TURN OFF THE MAIN SWITCH BY THE FRONT DOOR

Uncrew quite a long way the clamping screws to the left and right of the socket

Check that the two clamps will press against the correct part of the socket

Press firmly into place and tighten up the screws

A box of electrical tools is in the cupboard under the lounge window
