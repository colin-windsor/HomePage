16, La Bruyere, St Martin du Vieux Belleme, 61130, Normandy, France

The Roman Fort in Belleme Forest

A 1km walk up the road into the forest brings a track on your right that leads to the old Roman fort.

There is a notice board with all the details: "Ancient quatrilateral in earth: 72x124x82x130 m. Origin probably pre-historic, with possible Roman occupation."

Otherwise what you see is a big broad dyke in a roughly square shape.
