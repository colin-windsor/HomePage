16, La Bruyere, St Martin du Vieux Belleme, 61130, Normandy, France

The Druid's stone in the forest

This is not the most impressive ancient relic, but it is on the route of the nicest of cicular cycle rides from our house. Both directions are fine. Clockwise you ride up the hill into the forest. Turn East at the top of the hill at "Rond de Rendevous". Cross the horrid main road and carry on gently up hill until the big "Carrefour de sept bras". About 200m further on just at the top of the hill, there is a sign on the left saying "Pierre des Druids". Follow the path into the forest for about 50m and there it is on your left.

To continue the round trip, carry on down the hill until you meet the bigger route D5 from Mauves to Belleme, where you turn right. A hill takes you down out of the forrest and a slow uphill takes you past the big Agricultural Cooperative building to the road into Belleme.
