Bar des Halles at Mamers

This restaurent/bar is on the sunny side of Mammers main square. When eating indoors seems a waste of potential sun tan, this is a lovely place to sit outside and watch the Mammers world go by. We visited on 20/9/06 and had the €7 special salad dish. It was a large diameter plate filled with cod fish, shrimps, boiled egg, lettuce, tomato, carrots, and artichoke hearts. All with a lovely dressing. Very healthy eating! We did have to pay €2.60 for 50cl of still water!

Address: 54, Plave Carnot, 72600 Mamers.

Tel: 02 43 87 60 08
