16, La Bruyere, St Martin du Vieux Belleme, 61130, Normandy, France

Restaurant Eric HUBERT at Ige

We cycled here on my birthday 28/6/05. It is only 9 km or so from St Martin, along the nicest empty valley road. Take the first road on the right off the bypass after the supermarket.

The restaurant in unpretentious but with superb service and fine value. On the table was 1.5 litres of bottled water and a full bottle of wine. We had grated carrot with delicious vinegret source, a vast chicken leg with potatoes and mushrooms, a cheeseboard to grace the finest English restaurent, and Normandie tart of caramelised apples. Then came free coffee!

Address: 38, Rue du Mans,61130 IGE.

Tel: 02 33 83 22 01
