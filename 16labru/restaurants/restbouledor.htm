Restaurant Boule d'Or at Belleme

This would be our second nearest restaurent just up the hill to Belleme. We visited here on Thursday 14/5/09 after cycling to the Belleme market. It is situated on the Place de General Leclerc roundabout off Rue d'Alencon and opposite the police station and town park.

The restaurant is quite large with perhaps 30 tables and quite full when we went. The "Menu de jour" is 10 Euros including starter, main course, dessert and a 1/4 litre of wine. For starter Colin had a delicious fish mouse and Mo an egg mayonaise. For the main course Colin had a very large portion of chicken with chips. Mo had a beef stew with salad and chips. For dessert Colin had a gorgeous pear cream dish and Mo prunes in a liquor sauce.

Address: Karine and Patrick, Place de General Leclerc, Belleme, 61130.

Tel: 02 33 25 99 32.
