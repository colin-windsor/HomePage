The restaurant at Pervencheres

We cycled here on 24/4/7 taking a lovely route through the forest to La Perriere and then on the D275 with only a short session on the main D931. It took 1 hour 20mins.

The restaurant is just opposite the front door of the clean and lofty church which was open. Our meal was served by the owner Bernadette, and cost €10.50 for a salad starter, a cold meat dish, main course, cheese, dessert and a choice of a bottle of wine, cyder or bottled water.

Our main course was a ball of pork and sausage wrapped into a ball, originally tied up with string. It was served with a fine mushroom sauce and was delicious.

Address: 10, Rue de Courboyer, Place de L'Eglise, 61340 Noce.

Tel: 02 33 73 40 44
