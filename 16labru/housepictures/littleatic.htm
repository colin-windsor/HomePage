The little attic

When the new attic was built, we missed our place to store things that did not mind a bit of cold and damp. I made a hole in the mezannine bedroom ceiling. There is a beamed ceiling hidden above. I made a further hole through the straw and earth covering and there was our not-so-little attic! It must have been last touched in 1957 for there was a copy of the Parisiene of that date. You can see its cartoons in the bedroom below.
